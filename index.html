1. Introduction

Paw Around ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Phone Number: Used for account authentication via OTP verification
  • Display Name: Optional profile information you provide

Pet Information

  • Pet names, species, breed, and date of birth
  • Pet photos you upload
  • Vaccination records and care schedules
  • Grooming and tick/flea treatment schedules

Location Data

  • Your location to find nearby pet services (vets, groomers, pet shops, parks)
  • Location data for lost & found pet posts

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To track your pet's care schedules and send reminders
  • To show nearby pet services on the map
  • To enable lost & found pet posting in the community
  • To improve our app and user experience

4. Data Storage & Security

We use Firebase (by Google) to securely store your data. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication: For secure phone number login
  • Firebase Firestore: To store your pet and profile data
  • Firebase Storage: To store pet and post images
  • Google Maps: To display nearby pet services
  • Google Places API: To search for pet-related locations

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. Your data may be shared only:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • In lost & found posts (only information you choose to share publicly)

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data we have about you
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data from the app settings
  • Correct: Update your information at any time

8. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all your personal data, pet information, and uploaded images are permanently deleted.

9. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
