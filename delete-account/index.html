How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Paw Around app
  2. Go to the Profile tab (bottom right)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Enter your password/verify your identity
  5. Confirm deletion

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, phone number, profile photo)
  • All your pet profiles and photos
  • Vaccination and care records
  • Grooming and tick/flea schedules
  • Your community posts (lost & found)
  • All uploaded images
